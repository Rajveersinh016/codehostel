How do I earn referral rewards?

You earn rewards when your referred friends sign up using your referral code and complete their first booking. For each successful referral, you'll receive ‚Çπ500 credited to your Code Hostels wallet, which you can use for future bookings or withdraw to your bank account.

When will I receive my referral reward?

Referral rewards are processed within 7 days after your friend completes their first booking. Once processed, the reward amount will be added to your Code Hostels wallet, and you'll receive a notification via email.

Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?

There is no limit to the number of friends you can refer. The more people you refer, the more rewards you can earn! Additionally, referring 5 or more friends unlocks premium features and exclusive offers.

How long is my referral code valid?
